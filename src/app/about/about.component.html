About Us

Welcome to the page of Train Ticket Reservation System project, developed in collaboration with Anudip Foundation.

Project Overview

Our team, consisting of G Jhansi Lahari, N Mounika, and K Ganesh, in partnership with Anudip Foundation, is committed to providing a seamless and efficient train ticket reservation system. This project aims to enhance the user experience and streamline the process of booking train tickets for travelers.

Anudip Foundation

We are proud to collaborate with Anudip Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals through digital skills training and livelihood development. This project is a testament to our shared vision of leveraging technology for positive social impact.

Our Mission

At Anudip Foundation and our project team, our mission is to make train travel accessible and convenient for everyone. Through innovation and technology, we strive to simplify the ticket reservation process, making it user-friendly and efficient.
